Help

Controls

To look around the room, hold down the left mouse button and move the mouse around the screen. As you move the mouse, the camera will follow.

To click an object, hover the mouse over one of the clickable objects and click the left mouse button. Some objects will open an onscreen dialog while others will open up a new tab. Note that while hovering over a clickable object, text will appear over the object letting you know what content the object pertains to.

For sound, click the audio icon located in the upper-left corner of the screen. If you no longer wish to have the audio on, simply click the audio icon again.

Graphics

The onscreen graphics you see (or maybe do not see) require an underlying technology known as WebGL. WebGL allows for 3D content to be drawn in-browser. If the graphics look like they are drawn incorrectly or are simply not showing, check that your browser supports WebGL. Click here to troubleshoot WebGL issues.
